[**Uni Feedback**](https://uni-feedback.com) is a website where you can learn what courses are really like, through anonymous reviews from verified students.

- **Know what to expect before enrolling**: Avoid bad courses, find the best ones for you.
- **Share your own experience**: Help other students make informed decisions. Your review is always anonymous.

Check it out at **[uni-feedback.com](https://uni-feedback.com)**!
